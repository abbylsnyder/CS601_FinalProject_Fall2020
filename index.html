About The Pet Republic

The Pet Republic is committed to bringing humane, science based dog training to the people of Cambridge. As Pet Republicans, we are positive reinforcement dedicated. Our dog training curriculum is focused on constructive learning and helping our citizen dogs make positive choices. We adhere to the LIMA principle of Least Intrusive and Minimally Aversive training techniques. For more information about this philosophy, click HERE. It is our belief that dog training, while challenging, should be fun and engaging for both the animal and human student and lead to a deeper bond between dog and owner. We are highly skilled, knowledgeable animal professionals with years of dog handling experience and an on-going dedication to learning and continuing education. We hold ourselves to be stewards of our community, firm in the belief that good dog training makes for good neighbors and better neighborhoods.
